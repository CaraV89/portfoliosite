About Me

I am a freelance graphic designer who loves to help make client's visions become reality. With a degree, from Eastern Washington University, in Visual Communication and Design and a minor in Spanish. I am very passionate about the work I do and making sure it's the best quality work that I can produce. I am a very focused individual when it comes to projects that I work on. I love to take inspiration from clients, the world around me and designers that I find to be inspiring for me.

Work

"Fly Me to the Moon"

This piece was inspired by one of my all-time favorite singers, Frank Sinatra. I used a combination of five images to manipulate them into one image.

"Gestalt Abstraction"

This project was one of my favorite designs I created, strictly inspired by a son called, "10,000 Emerald Pools" by the BORNS. I used previously created shapes from an image trace and created an abstract pattern on an edited image.

"Propaganda"

This design was created as a propaganda piece, using multiple typefaces to create one large typeface. The message is brought across directly and neatly.
